Animation: pembuatan Animation terdapat pada key frame.

- Nilai yang bisa dimasukan dalam property Animation:
= name, duration, timing-function, delay, iteration-count (angka || infinite), direction (normal, reverse, alternate, alternate-reverse), fill-mode (none, forwards, backwards, both), play-state (running, paused).

Contoh propertinya:

@keyframes [name]{
    from{
        [property-css]
    }
    to{
        [property-css]
    }
}
